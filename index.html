⭐ THE 4 HERO GOALS
👂
LISTENING & FOLLOWING DIRECTIONS
when a grown-up says something, you do it
WHAT THIS MEANS:
When mom, dad, or your teacher tells you to do something — like "time to put shoes on" or "come sit down" — you stop what you're doing and give it a try. Even if you don't want to, heroes try!
💎 HERO EXAMPLE:
Mom says "time to turn off Minecraft." Instead of saying NO, you say "okay" and turn it off. BOOM — diamond earned!
🦺
STAYING SAFE & CLOSE BY
your body stays where grown-ups can see you
WHAT THIS MEANS:
You stay in safe places where your grown-up can always see you. No running into the street, running away from the group, or going somewhere without telling an adult first. Being safe means being seen!
💎 HERO EXAMPLE:
At the store, instead of running to another aisle, you walk right next to mom and hold the cart. Your grown-up gives you a thumbs up — diamond earned!
🤲
SAFE HANDS & BODY
your body is kind to everyone around you
WHAT THIS MEANS:
We keep our hands, feet, and body to ourselves. No hitting, kicking, pushing, or grabbing — even if we feel really mad or really excited. Our body is strong, so we use it to help, not hurt.
💎 HERO EXAMPLE:
Your friend has a toy you want. Instead of grabbing it, you say "can I have a turn?" and wait. You used your words like a total HERO — diamond earned!
🧘
USING CALM-DOWN TOOLS
when big feelings come, you use your superpowers
WHAT THIS MEANS:
Sometimes our body feels like it has TOO MUCH energy or we get really upset. Instead of exploding, we use a calm-down tool — like taking deep breaths, squeezing something soft, jumping 10 times, or asking for a hug.
💎 HERO EXAMPLE:
You feel really mad because it's time to stop playing. You take 3 big deep breaths like a Creeper slowly exhaling... and your body calms down. LEGENDARY — diamond earned!
